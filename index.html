Mountain Bike Research Lab

Intro

Between the Tape is a small mountain bike research lab conducting data driven mountain bike journalism. All our methods, data, and code are open-source.

Articles

  • Are Lower Tire Pressures Faster?: an experiment to assess how mountain bike tire pressures effect performance.
  • What Could Have Been: simulating the 2024 DH World Cup season, without mistakes.

Projects

  • downhillr: an R package that scrapes, cleans, and exports downhill mountain bike race results.
